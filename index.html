About

The UI/UX Hackathon is a space where ideas transform into meaningful digital experiences. Designers collaborate to craft intuitive, visually engaging solutions under time pressure. It's a challenge that pushes creativity beyond limits.

Guidelines

Team Size
Each team must consist of exactly three members.
01.
Design Tools
Use any design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
02.
Time Limit
Complete your design within the allocated time frame.
03.
Presentation
Prepare a 5-minute pitch to present your solution.
04.
Originality
All designs must be original work created during the event.
